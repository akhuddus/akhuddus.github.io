Recent Past Projects

  • Cisco Smart Call HOME

Developed Cisco network diagnostics and automated remediation solutions for wide range of Cisco network gear. The developed solution receives and processes diagnostic events, either ondemand or event driven from customer data centers and processes them to either to alert the Network Operations Command center for intervention OR by locating and executing the automated remediation procedures for network recovery and resiliency. Having Smart Call Home enabled devices in the data centers, customers not only gets the visibility into the inventory, health and compliance information but also the warranty replacements of faulty components are handled without manual intervention.

  • Cisco Workload automation

Workload solution automates the enterprise job scheduling with centralized management and orchestration. Developed solution enables the users to define wide range of jobs through GUI, add conditions, set priorities and deadlines. It creates the execution plan, locates and runs the jobs on available pooled resources in the data center, captures and stores the result for audit and troubleshooting.

Cisco and other enterprise customers uses the platform to automate and run their workloads for variety of use cases: Backups, scheduled tasks, payrol processing, sales data consolidations, bigdata and analytics jobs,..etc.,

  • Cisco Workflow automation

Workflow solution automates the orchestration of services to enable the digital transformation across the enterprise. Organizations in an enterprise make their services available through a service catalog. Consumers discover, integrate and use them through Zero Touch Depoloyment process.
